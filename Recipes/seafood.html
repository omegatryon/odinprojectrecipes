Savannah Seafood Stuffing

This dish is a little more complicated than the previous one! thankfully for you, we have all the steps!

Ingrediants needed

  • ½ cup margarine
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 1 pound crabmeat, drained and flaked
  • ½ pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
  • ½ cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
  • 1 (6 ounce) package corn bread stuffing mix
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, divided
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth

    • Like the steps required, the ingrediants follows suit with all that you need. Once you gather the list feel free to scroll down and follow our step by step instructions.

    Step by step instructions

    1. Melt the margarine in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the bell pepper, onion, celery crabmeat and shrimp; cook and stir for about 5 minutes.
    2. Set aside. In a large bowl, stir together the stuffing, bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon of sugar.
    3. Mix in the vegetables and seafood from the skillet.
    4. Stir in the cream of mushroom soup and as much of the chicken broth as you like. Spoon into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    5. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly toasted on top.
